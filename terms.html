General

Company Name is a new client that will be receiving a complete build out of their core network infrastructure. A discovery meeting with Matt Mishalow was used to compile the results of this solution.

Servers

Company Name currently has no physical servers at any location. They currently use Data Resolutions which host a VM for Dynamics with four seats. We will prep O365 Dynamics and migrate data in from Data Resolution.

Workstations

Company Name office is currently operating with a mixture of 15” and 13” MacBook Air/Pro’s and a few Windows 10 units. These workstations are owned out right by Company Name. Long Range IT will install an RMM tool 40 each desktop or laptop.

Email & Security

Long Range IT will create a new Office 365 tenant and prep the environment for migration. There will be a mixture of Office Enterprise E5, E3 and E1 licenses. We will migrate all mailboxes from HushMail into O365. We will create Office 365 Groups in place of Distribution Groups. Each mailbox will be enabled with The Email Laundry Email Security and ATP license.

Backup & Disaster Recovery

Company Name currently has not backup and disaster recovery procedure in place. LRIT will backup all O365 emails. LRIT will also backup 10 laptops designated by GDI.

Network Backbone

Long Range IT will install new Sophos XG135 firewalls at HQ. The network backbone for Company Name will consists of 100/1000 switch. If the location does not currently have a 100/1000 switch, a replacement will be ordered where needed.

Telco & Internet

VoIP is provided by InterMedia and internet is provided by Cox. An audit will be done with recommendations for cost saving solutions for consideration.

Cut Over

This solution will be coordinated between Company Name and the Long Range IT Project team.

SUMMARY OF SUPPORT & REQUIREMENTS

Support

LRIT's technical support center answers service calls 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Our help desk is staffed with experienced technicians 24x7x365. Our managed service agreement clients also benefit from remote support, whereby our technicians utilize remote access tools to connect to your office systems, allowing the ability to diagnose hardware and software failures via dedicated Internet connections. All of our service agreement clients receive priority service.

Requirements

LRIT will require all schematics, drawings, configuration data and easy access to all facility locations. All work possible will be performed during regular business hours. We will try to minimize disruption while performing server migration.

Exclusions

This proposal does not include replacement of or parts required for repairs on printers, screens or peripherals, (PDAs, point of sale scanners, digital cameras, cell phones, Blackberrys or any other specialized accessory), unless this equipment was originally provided under this agreement or a pre-existing agreement. All labor required for installation and/or repair of the above devices is covered under this agreement. Consumables such as printer maintenance kits, toner, ink, batteries, paper, etc. are not included or covered under this service agreement and will be invoiced separately.

Project Fees

The estimated project fee for the initial migration will be $10,500. A 50% deposit will be required in the amount of $5,250 to kick off the migration schedule. Once the initial migration is completed, we can provide a more accurate monthly MSP fee.
